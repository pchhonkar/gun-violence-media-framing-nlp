A mass shooting at a Texas elementary school killed 19 children and two teachers Tuesday and the suspected gunman was killed,Gov. Greg Abbott said.
Abbott identified the suspect as Salvador Ramos, a Uvalde resident, who is also dead and acted alone, authorities said. He had ahandgun and possibly a rifle when he opened fire at Robb Elementary School, he said. Two police officers were shot and woundedbut were expected to survive, Abbott said. 
"Texans across the state are grieving for the victims of this senseless crime and for the community of Uvalde. Cecilia and I mournthis horrific loss and we urge all Texans to come together to show our unwavering support to all who are suffering," he said in astatement. 
The shooter was likely killed by responding officers, but the investigation was still ongoing, authorities said.
